FLIGHT ATTENDANT

ABOUT THE JOB

My dream career is a flight attendant. I've wanted to become a flight attendant since I love to travel but don't like how much it cost. Flight attendants help keep people on the plane safe, make sure every passenger is following the rules, and secure the plane. I could see myself in this career because when I want to do something, I will dedicate myself to it. I am a responsible person, who is disciplined and even though I might not have all the qualities needed right now, I can shape myself into them later on.
