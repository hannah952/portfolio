About the University

DePaul University is a university located in Chicago, Illinois. It currently has about 21,000 students attending the school. According to the website, over 70% of students live on campus and 60% of students complete internships. The school has a 74% graduation rate and a 69% acceptance rate. The tuition is about $42,012.

Requirements to apply

  • You need to be above average in high school classes
  • Minimum of 2.8 GPA
  • Have to had taken the SAT/ACT

Amenities

  • Clean Campus
  • Laundry is free
  • Vending machines all over the school
  • Lots of places near it

Reason for Attending

They are one of the best colleges for a hospitality, communications, and education degree which is what airlines desire.
