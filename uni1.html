About the College

Cypress College is a college located in Cypress, California. It is a community college and the tuition is about $27,605. Meaning about around $7,000 a year. The acceptance rate is 100%, meaning it is not selective at all. The graduation rate is 24% on the other hand. However, the school has many alumni who are flight attendants or have experience with things like that.

Requirements to apply

  • Needs to have graduated highschool or certificate of high school proficiency
  • Have a GPA above 2.0
  • Complete the application process

Amenities

  • Flight attendant program is said to be one of the best
  • The campus is nice
  • Doesn't cost as much money as it is a community college

Reason for Attending

Their flight attendant program is good, and it's said that they produce many flight attendants and that the program is there to prepare you for what it's like and to receive tips from professionals.
